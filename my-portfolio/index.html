About Me

My name is Jason Portilla, and I am 31 years old. I am originally from New York, but Apopka has been my home since I was 12 years old. I graduated from Apopka High School in 2003. I then attended the University of Central Florida where I earned my Bachelor's Degree in Criminal Justice.

Once I graduated in 2009, I pursued a career as a Police Officer for the Orlando Police Department. I have been a Police Officer for 8 years. During these years, I was assigned to several areas throughout the city patrolling neighborhoods and building a relationship with the community. I have volunteered to help kids in the community and guide them towards a positive direction.

During my career, my passion for computers was always in my mind. Computers has always been something I wanted to pursue. An opportunity came when I discovered a course offered through the University of Central Florida. This was my opportunity, so I jumped.
